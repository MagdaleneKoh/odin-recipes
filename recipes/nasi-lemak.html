Nasi Lemak

Home

A beautiful dish of Nasi Lemak with fragrant coconut rice paired with a unique blend of Sambal.

The side dishes were also phenomenal. The chicken wing was savoury, juicy and crunchy, while the anchovies were salty, crunchy yet felt clean at the same time.

This lovely dish can be found at a humble food court in the Central Business District.

Here is the list of ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken wing
  • Sambal
  • Acah
  • Cucumber
  • Anchovies
  • Peanut
  • Banana leaf

Steps to making your very own nasi lemak

  1. Cook fragrant rice
  2. Blend spices for Sambal and allow it to be slowly cooked
  3. Marinate chicken wing and deep fry
  4. Fry an egg
  5. Place it all together on a plate
