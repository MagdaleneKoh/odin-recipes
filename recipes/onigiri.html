A simple yet divine rice ball.

The salty, sweet and sour rice ball that is wrapped by fragrant seaweed would satiate any hunger pangs quickly.

This humble rice ball called the onigiri can be made easily and in advance to be taken as a main dish or a snack.

Here is the list of ingredients:

  • Short grain rice
  • Rice vinegar mixed with salt and sugar
  • Dried seaweed

Steps to making your very own Onigiri

  1. Cook japanese short grain rice
  2. Mix vinegar with salt and sugar
  3. Incorporate mixed vinegar into rice
  4. Wrap rice with cut dried seaweed
  5. Serve with pickles and miso soup
